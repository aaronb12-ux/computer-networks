### rdt2.1: Receiver, Handling Garbled ACK/NAKs

Whenever you get a packet that is not corrupted, the receiver always sends an acknowledgement. A NAK (Negative Acknowledgement) is reserved for corrupted packets only. (NAK is only sent on corruption)
